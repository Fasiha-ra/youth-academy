Youth Academy Frequently Asked Questions

Have a Questions

Q: Do you offer live lectures?

A: Yes, our courses include interactive live lectures.

Q: How can I access live lectures?

A: Live lectures are accessible through our Youth Academy Mobile App and Website.

Q: Can I ask questions during live lectures?

A: Yes, our live lectures are interactive.

Q: Are live lectures recorded?

A: Yes, live lectures are recorded and available for future access.

Q: How do I join a live lecture?

A: Students receive a unique link to join live lectures via the student dashboard after enrollment.

Q: What if I miss a live lecture?

A: Recordings will be available in the student dashboard.

Q: Are live lectures scheduled at fixed times?

A: Yes, different batches have different timings, generally at night shift.

Q: Are lectures recorded?

A: Yes, all live lectures are recorded for future access.

Q: How can I access recorded lectures?

A: Recorded lectures are available in the student dashboard or Youth Academy Mobile App.

Q: For how long are recorded lectures available?

A: Recorded lectures are available till the last exams of your batch.

Q: Can I download recorded lectures?

A: Yes, you can view and download recorded lectures.

Q: Are recorded lectures accessible on mobile devices?

A: Yes, our recorded lectures are mobile-friendly and available on the Youth Academy Application.

Payment Process

  1. Select your preferred payment method.
  2. Follow the payment instructions.
  3. For offline payments, upload a screenshot of the transaction receipt.

Screenshot Upload

To confirm offline payments, please upload a screenshot of your transaction receipt:

  1. Login to your student dashboard.
  2. Click on "Make Payment" or "Upload Payment Screenshot."
  3. Select the payment method and upload the screenshot.
  4. Our team will verify the payment and update your account status.

Payment Verification

Our team will verify payments within 60 minutes. Once verified:

  1. You will receive a payment confirmation SMS on your phone number.
  2. Your course access will be granted.

Q: How will I access the course?

A: After payment, you will receive your login credentials on your registered phone number.

Q: What credentials will I receive?

A: You will receive a username and password to access the course.

Q: How long does it take to receive login credentials?

A: For live payments, credentials are sent immediately. In the case of offline payments, it usually takes 60 minutes to verify.

Q: What if I don't receive my login credentials?

A: Contact our support team at WhatsApp: 0342-1016201.

Q: Can I access the course on multiple devices?

A: Yes, you can access the course on multiple devices using your login credentials.

Login Credentials Instructions
